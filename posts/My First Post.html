Finally.. I've done it! I've created myself a blog.

Why a blog?

I never really though I would be the person to ever create a blog. When thinking about a blog I have always thought of the barbie/pink bloggers that i've been so traumatized from.(Having a twin sister will do that.) However, I figured it was finally time to give it a shot. It was time to get my thoughts and experiences out there. This blog will be about everything security, from my personal projects to interesting things I get to experience in my day to day work. I welcome you to join me in my ventures, my thoughts and my ideas.
